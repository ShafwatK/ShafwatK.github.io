Slatwall

Designed and fabricated a custom slatwall to maximize space usage by using verticle space. Applied LEAN principles as well as the 6 sigmas in design process.

Welding Fan

Calculated the CFM requirements of a exhaustion fan for a welding booth. Picked out an appropriate fan, designed a custom mount for it, fabricated, and installed it.

Laser Cable Management

Unmaintained cable management was causing workflow inefficiencies in the laser cutting section at Sable Metal. Designed and fabricated a custom cable management system to improve workflow and safety. Considered manufacturability as well as ease and price of assembly in the design process.

Assembly Optimization

Documented the assembly process of several different products at Sable Metal, most of them being doors. Created instructions and detailed assembly drawings to help maximize efficiency and reduce errors. Modified existing parts in order to help with efficiency in assembly.

Arduino Shop Alarm

Replaced the existing shop alarm system at Sable Metal with a custom Arduino based system. The existing system was old and broken, the new system is more reliable and easier to use, it also allows for remote control.
